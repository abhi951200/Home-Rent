Home

A house is a building that is made for people to live in. It is usually built for a family (parents and their children).[1] It is a "permanent" building that is meant to stay standing.

It is not easily packed up and carried away like a tent, or moved like a caravan. If people live in the same house for more than a short stay, then they call it their "home". Being without a home is called homelessness. Houses are numbered.
